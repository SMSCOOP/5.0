IME Remit

At IME, creating innovations, unleashing new values and delivering service excellence to the stakeholders are not just catchphrases; they are the core of our very existence.” With a legacy of more than 2 decades, IME Limited is the pioneer company in the business of remittance solutions providers in Nepal, specializing in the delivery of world class money transfer services to families and friends from around the globe. With a workforce of more than 5000 people from diverse backgrounds and cultures, we are here to deliver the best service as we understand and respect your money transfer needs. Having served millions of satisfied customers, IME takes pride in transferring happiness to people around the world through its services. And, we will continue to do so. Today, IME has global reach, thanks to our worldwide business partners present across the globe enabling swift delivery of remittance services from more than 200 countries and networks spanned over 1 million locations around the world.

City Express

A unified group with diversified businesses, among which City Express Money Transfer (CEMT) is generally regarded as the face of the group. Established in the July of 2007, City Express Money Transfer (CEMT) is today recognized as the leading remittance company in Nepal and overseas. A pioneer in Nepal’s remittance sector for over a decade and is part of the prestigious group of companies City Express Group. With over 13 years of experience in remittance, CEMT has a collective of over 8000 payout locations spread over Nepal. Along with this, CEMT also operates in a global scale with services catering worldwide. CEMT has been able to create its unique identification by becoming one of the fastest growing remittance companies in Nepal. Besides CEMT, City Express Group has diversified its business in financial sector, information technology, Share trade, travel and tourism, hospitality, visa processing, retails and so forth. We are a dedicated team committed to provide excellent customer service.

Prabhu Money Transfer

Prabhu Money Transfer P. Ltd (Prabhu) is a leading remittance company registered in Nepal and having many international branches/agents in different parts of the Asia and North America. Prabhu is a very popular and reputed money transfer organization among the Nepalese in Malaysia, Qatar, Saudi Arabia, Kuwait, UAE, USA and other countries. Prabhu is reputable and well-known brand name (synonym) of remittance for Nepal among Nepalese Labors and residents in Foreign Countries.

Moneygram
